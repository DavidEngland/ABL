Pr = 1; take identical stability functions for momentum and heat with constant L:
\[
\phi_m(\zeta)=\phi_h(\zeta)=\phi(\zeta)=1 + a\,\zeta,\quad a=0.47,\ \ \zeta=\frac{z}{L}.
\]

Gradient Richardson number
\[
Ri_g(\zeta)=\zeta\,\frac{\phi_h}{\phi_m^2}=\frac{\zeta}{\phi(\zeta)}=\frac{\zeta}{1+a\zeta}.
\]

Derivatives in ζ
\[
\frac{dRi_g}{d\zeta}=\frac{1}{(1+a\zeta)^2},\qquad
\frac{d^2 Ri_g}{d\zeta^2}=\frac{-2a}{(1+a\zeta)^3}.
\]

Neutral curvature (ζ→0)
\[
\Delta=V_{\log}(0)=a-2a=-a,\qquad \left.\frac{d^2 Ri_g}{d\zeta^2}\right|_{0}=2\Delta=-2a=-0.94.
\]

Height-coordinate forms (constant L)
\[
\frac{dRi_g}{dz}=\frac{1}{L}\,\frac{1}{\big(1+a\,z/L\big)^2},\qquad
\frac{d^2 Ri_g}{dz^2}=\frac{1}{L^2}\,\frac{-2a}{\big(1+a\,z/L\big)^3}.
\]
